Cypress Point Country Club

(757) 490-8822

5340 Clubhead Road

Virginia Beach, Virginia 23455

Enjoy the country club lifestyle in the heart of the City! Located within minutes of the Norfolk International Airport, the Virginia Beach Town Center shopping and dining complex, and the beaches of the Chesapeake Bay, Cypress Point Country Club is perfectly located for individuals and families who want to enjoy the conveniences of a city address yet the pace of a suburban lifestyle. A nine time winner of the Virginian Pilot’s “Best of the Best” award, Cypress Point offers a terrific Ault & Clark designed golf course, both casual and fine dining, and an junior Olympic sized swimming pool for full family enjoyment! While limited public access is available on the golf course, memberships are available that offer use of all the club’s amenities, privileged access to all six Hampton Roads Golf Clubs, and all club social events and family activities. Contact Jonathan Faw at (757) 490-6300 for information on how to become a privileged member.
